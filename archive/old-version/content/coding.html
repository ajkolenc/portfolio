Technologies/Platforms

Through my game development career I have worked with numerous different technologies and platforms, Home Improvisation being a major example of many of these technologies.

Virtual Reality

I have extensive experience with the Oculus Rift and HTC Vive, both of which I supported for Home Improvisation. This included working directly the the input and rendering APIs for both platforms, and creating a layer of abstraction to enable interchanging the systems with minimal code duplication.

I started working in VR with the original Oculus Rift development kit, and participated in the 2013 VR Jam (see video). This familiarity with the affordances of VR as a medium allowed me to make a quality VR port of Home Improvisation, and has continued to shape my perspective as I continue making games in VR.
